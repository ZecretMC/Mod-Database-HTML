About

This is a mod downloader. Not much different from CurseForge, I know. I just wanted to make an easy gui that is easier than CurseForge's. This downloader also supports OptiFine and Forge downloads, so I guess it is different. It's still a wip, so if you have questions or bug reports, contact me on the project's discord!
